About Trixie

Trixie is a smart Telegram bot that automatically reacts to every message in your groups or private chats using a variety of fun emojis. It keeps conversations lively, engaging, and full of surprises!

Features

Automatic Reactions

Reacts instantly to every message, making your chat more interactive and fun.

Random Emojis

Each reaction is unique with a wide collection of emojis!

Easy Deployment

Run Trixie on your local machine or VPS with tmux easily.

Lightweight

Minimal resource usage ensures smooth performance without slowing down your chats.

Add Trixie to Your Group

How It Works

Trixie listens to all messages in a chat and reacts automatically with fun, animated emojis. Perfect for groups that want to stay active and entertained without lifting a finger!
